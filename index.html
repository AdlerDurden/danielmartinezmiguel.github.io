About me, the guy. 

I hail from the sunny city of Murcia, Spain. From an early age, my fascination with video games ignited, and I became an unabashed nerd—though I prefer the term “enthusiast.” 

Time has flowed like rain since I inherited my uncle’s vintage NES console. Now, as a fully functional adult nerd, my passion for video games and technology remains unwavering. I immerse myself in a diverse array of gaming experiences, from spine-tingling horror titles to intricate strategy games. Yet, it’s within the realms of RPGs and shooters that my heart truly finds its home.

Beyond the pixels, I find joy in analog pursuits like board games, tabletop RPGs, and meticulously painting Warhammer miniatures transport me to imaginative worlds. 

And when I step outdoors, I’m drawn to team sports—football being a favorite. 
Optimism and an easygoing nature define me. Life is an adventure, and I’m here for the ride!
